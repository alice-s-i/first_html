easiest brownies

Ingredients:

dark chocolate — like around 150-180g
butter — the same amount as chocolate
3 eggs —
flour — 3 tablespoons or somewhere around 75g
sugar — around 200 g
salt - ½ tablespoon

the key to making the best brownies is using the best chocolate you can find. you’ll never EVER get great results making brownies (and desserts in general) with some chocolate that’s half decent. people tend to think that the best recipes involve many complex ingredients when in reality you just need a few good ones.

How to Make It:

melt your dark chocolate with butter. please DO NOT overheat it, bain-marie can help you with that. In a separate bowl mix eggs with sugar but don’t do it for too long, we don't need an over whipped mixture since we want our brownies to be thin and moist. when melted chocolate isn’t as hot anymore, slowly pour it into the egg mixture, flour goes last. pour your mixture onto a baking dish (use parchment paper greased with butter) and bake at 200℃ for around 30 mins but always check it with a stick or a toothpick (if you stick it and it comes out dry – it’s ready), but again do NOT overbake it, we still kinda want it moist and everything.
Also you have to try it with ice–cream – it’s so so so good🍫
