cinnamon pear crumble

Ingredients:

3 pears — any type is fine as long as you like them
flour — 5 tablespoons
sugar — 3 tablespoons, you can use brown sugar just as well
species — cinnamon, you can also try ginger, cardamom and nutmeg
oats — 5 tablespoons
butter - around 100 g

extras:

almond flour — idk, just add couple of tablespoons for some extra smell and almond flavor
sugar and cinnamon - a specific amount of pure vibes to add to pears
ice-cream - vanilla would be perfect

How to Make It:

cut pears into slices, mix them in a bowl with as much sugar and cinnamon as you want. in a separate bowl mix dry ingredients (flour, almond flour, sugar, species, oats), add cold butter slices into the mix and try grinding it with the dry ingredients. put the pears on a baking dish (use parchment paper greased with butter), add the “dough” on top of it. bake it until the top looks golden brown, than serve it with ice-cream🍐
